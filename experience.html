Overview

I am a consulting account manager with 9 years of experience working across Africa, Europe, Asia, and America. I possess demonstrable skills in business development, project management, data analysis, and information technology, and have experience working both in-office and remotely.

I have extensive experience in accounts, project, and operations management within the Information Technology, Infrastructure, and Corporate Services industries. As a people person and team player, I am highly motivated to provide genuine value to clients through customized service delivery and solutions. I possess comprehensive knowledge of my profession and remain up-to-date on current global trends and market conditions.

Dedicated to keeping my clients and organization on the cutting edge of innovation related to new technology and best practices with a passion for consulting to identify operational inefficiencies, architecting process enhancements, inspiring buy-in from cross-functional teams, and driving implementations to improve client/colleague success, optimize workflows and increase productivity.

As a technology enthusiast, I am dedicated to learning and understanding new technologies such as artificial intelligence and web application development. I am also professionally versed in a number of contemporary professional softwares, including Office365, CRM systems, ChatGPT, Adobe Premiere Pro, and others.

I am a lifelong learner, always seeking opportunities for self-improvement and acquisition of new knowledge. Currently, I am enrolled in a Full Stack Development bootcamp to further enhance my skills and knowledge.
